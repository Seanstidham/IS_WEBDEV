Persona 5 Royal

This is a game that I have been playing a lot in my free time, I highly recommend playing it to anyone that hasnt.

Persona 5 Royal is a tactical turnbase RPG that has a ton of fun features including:

  1. A fun, engaging story
  2. A character fusion system
    • Build the strongest Persona for any situation
    • Heres the website on how to make the exact one you want
  3. And an absolutely amazing Soundtrack, heres a taste of it

In Conclusion

Persona 5 Royal is a great expansion to the original Persona 5 game that adds many quality of life improvements and better mechanics and is a must play for turnbase RPG fans
